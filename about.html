About Mani Mughal

Mani Mughal is a 27-year-old Electrical Engineer from Colombo, Sri Lanka. He travels around Sri Lanka on weekends and started a blog to share his experiences. As a lot of misinformation about travel in Sri Lanka persist, he challenges himself to clear up those myths.

"For a 27-year-old, I'm pretty sure that I have seen quite some things around the world already and made my fair share of trips. Those trips made me the person I am today and also created my passion for travelling. You might question why an Electrical Engineer is trying to teach the world about travelling; my answer to the masses is that I want to present travellers with the best possible experience travelling around Sri Lanka. 'We travel, some of us forever, to seek other places, other lives, other souls.' — I know I know, quite a cheesy and deep quote at the same time. How can you know who you are and where your belief is if you have never experienced other cultures, other views from completely different people from various ethnicities? I hope that writing this blog can get you, whoever you are, excited about travelling, or at least open your mind a little bit and give you a bit more information about some beautiful destinations in Sri Lanka."
